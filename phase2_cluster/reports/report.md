Silhouette is a gentle plateau (~0.28) from k=12-20 — typical for short-text embeddings with no crisp cluster boundaries — and **peaks at k=15** (KMeans 0.293). It falls off below k=12 (themes too broad) and above k=25 (clusters fragment). We therefore choose **k=15**, slightly coarser than the 20 labelled root issues: at this granularity some narrow issues merge (e.g. card-not-working with declined-payment), which the gold co-clustering below quantifies.

### Final clustering (KMeans, k=15)

- **Gold same-cluster rate: 87% overall** (easy 90%, medium 90%, hard 80%).
- Cluster sizes: largest 55, median 24, smallest 17 queries.
- Silhouette: 0.293.
